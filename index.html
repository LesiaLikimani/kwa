This is KWA

Kerarapon Water Association (KWA) is a community-based organization dedicated to ensuring reliable, safe, and sustainable water access for the residents of Kerarapon. We work together with local stakeholders to protect our water sources, improve distribution systems, and promote responsible water use. Through transparency, community engagement, and continuous development, KWA strives to provide every household with dependable water services while safeguarding our environment for future generations.

Our main objectives

Develop and Sustain a Clean Water System

We work to establish and continuously improve a reliable, safe, and efficient water system that serves the needs of the Kerarapon community.

Provide Safe and Accessible Water to Members

KWA is committed to delivering clean, treated water to all members, ensuring consistent access for household and community use.

Manage and Regulate Water Supply Responsibly

We maintain and oversee water distribution networks, ensuring proper regulation, fair usage, and sustainable management of available resources.

Promote Environmental Conservation and Well-Being

KWA protects the Kerarapon Springs catchment area, fosters a clean environment, and promote practices that improve overall living standards.

Learn About KWA

Kerarapon Water Association is a non-profit, community-based organization dedicated to developing, operating, and sustaining a clean and reliable water supply system for residents of Kerarapon Sub-location. Guided by strong values of accountability, integrity, and service, the Association manages water sources, oversees distribution infrastructure, promotes environmental protection around Kerarapon Springs, and ensures members receive safe, piped water. KWA also mobilizes resources, collaborates with partners, and empowers the community through awareness and education aimed at improving health and living standards.

Our pride

Archivements

KWA has fully upgraded from traditional mechanical meters to advanced smart meters, bringing transparency, accuracy, and real-time monitoring to our members. This major technological leap has reduced disputes, improved efficiency, and positioned KWA as a modern, forward-thinking water service provider.

Modernized Our Water Billing Through Smart Meter Technology

Our commitment to reliable service, improved infrastructure, and community engagement has attracted a 40% increase in new members. This steady growth reflects the trust that Kerarapon residents place in KWA as their preferred water service provider, and it strengthens our ability to continue improving services for the entire community.

40% Growth in Our Customer Base Over Three Years

To ensure dependable water access all year round, KWA has expanded its water sources beyond the Kerarapon Spring. We have successfully drilled two high-yield boreholes, increasing supply stability, especially during dry seasons. This investment secures the community’s future and reduces over-reliance on a single source.

Strengthened Water Security with New Boreholes

We have invested in an additional 32-cubic-metre (32,000-litre) storage tank to enhance our water holding capacity. This upgrade ensures more consistent distribution, reduced shortages, and improved service reliability for all KWA members.

Expanded Storage Capacity with a New 32,000-Litre Tank

Through network upgrades, enhanced monitoring, and better maintenance practices, we have reduced non-revenue water from 38% down to 20%. This achievement means more of the water we produce reaches our members—leading to better service delivery, improved efficiency, and greater financial sustainability.

Significant Reduction in Non-Revenue Water

In partnership with the CRBS and the Kerarapon community, KWA played a key role in the successful tarmacking of the local community road. This initiative has improved accessibility, boosted local development, and strengthened our relationship with the residents we serve.

Community Development Through Improved Road Infrastructure
